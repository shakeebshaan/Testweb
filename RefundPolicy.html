Strong Start Terms Refund Policy
Last Updated: 20-2-2025
1. General Terms
1.1. Payments must be made in full before any plans or consultations are provided.
1.2. Fees are non-refundable unless explicitly stated otherwise in writing.
2. Refund Eligibility
2.1. Refunds will only be considered in cases where a written agreement states refund eligibility.
2.2. If a user terminates their program with prior notice, refunds (if applicable) will be processed based on unused services in accordance with this policy.
2.3. Misrepresentation of weight, medical conditions, or other key information may lead to program termination without a refund.
2.4. Refunds will not be provided for early termination due to user non-compliance or inappropriate behavior.
3. Refund Processing
3.1. Approved refunds will be processed within 14 days from the date of payment.
3.2. Details of the refund amount and processing method will be communicated via email or the designated communication medium.
4. Contact
For any refund-related inquiries, users should contact their personal manager or support team.
